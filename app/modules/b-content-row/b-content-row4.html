DERMAL FILLERS

Dermal fillers are a hugely effective non-surgical means for enhancing features and treating wrinkles. Effective almost instantly, fillers can redefine features, smooth away your wrinkles and restore your skin’s volume, providing a smooth natural look and feel.

  • Suitable for: nasolabial lines, peri-oral lines, forehead lines, frown lines
  • Cost: from £200 per 1ml of filler
  • Treatment time: up to 30 minutes
  • Results: effective instantly
  • Duration: 6-12 months
