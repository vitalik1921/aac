ANTI-WRINKLE TREATMENTS

Our anti-wrinkle treatments use Botulinum Toxin - often known as Botox® - to help combat the signs of ageing, by relaxing the muscles that cause unwanted lines and wrinkles.

This treatment is most suitable for areas around the eyes and forehead - and will smooth and lift the skin in just 2-4 weeks post treatment.

The discomfort with anti-wrinkle injections is very minimal and the treatment will have no negative effect on your daily routine.

  • Suitable for: forehead, frown lines, crows feet
  • Cost: 1 area £100, 2 areas £170, 3 areas £230
  • Treatment time: up to 30 minutes
  • Results: effective within 2-4 weeks
  • Duration: 3-6 months
